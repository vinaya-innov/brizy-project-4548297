Registration Guidelines for Participating Parents and Children

  1. UY, MY & MY 2 participants must be accompanied by their parents to the ashram.

2. Only those kids can do the course whose parents are doing Advance Meditation Program in the Ashram.

3. Parent and children will be given accommodation in the same room. One room for one family.

4. UY/MY1/ MY2 courses will be taken with proper social distancing but if children, in their enthusiasm break it, the teacher, volunteer or Ashram shall not be held responsible for the same.

5. Each course batch will not be more than 15 students.

6. The following forms need to be duly filled and signed by the parents on arrival:

a. Children and Teens Program Registration form

b. Consent form affirming that parents are fully aware of the risks involved in the current COVID pandemic and are ready to let their child participate in the Ashram Courses and follow proper sanitization guidelines.

c. Declaration form stating that after going home if anyone from the family is tested Covid positive; then ashram, the organizer, volunteer or AOL teachers shall not be held responsible for the same.

d. Additionally, the participant and the parent must provide a medical certificate confirming that no one in their family was tested COVID positive in the last 20 days

7. If within 10 days of leaving the Ashram, the participant or the parent is tested positive, then they should immediately inform the Ashram and Teachers so that all other participants could be cautioned about the same.

8. We discourage participants who have cough/cold or any respiratory problems like asthma to register during these times

9. Wearing mask throughout the stay is mandatory.

10. Participant must carry their own masks/ face shield and sanitizer and must keep sanitizing themselves on a regular basis.

11. The responsibility of waking up the child, getting him/her ready, having food and getting them to the program venue is solely on the parents.

12. If at any point any participant gets cough or cold during the course then the participant will be immediately made to self-isolate and may even have to leave the Ashram premises as per the guidelines of the ashram

13. In case any symptoms develop, credit note will be given to the participant

14. Kindly bring your immunity kit and medicines and take it daily (one can buy it from Ashram if required)

15. All the rooms will be well sanitized before allotting to a family; a clean set of blankets, bedsheets etc will be provided in every room. However, if you wish to get your own blankets, you are free to do so.

16. Since it is winters, request you to take all possible precautions in terms of getting warm clothes, moisturizing creams, lip guard, shoes, socks etc. as per your requirement.

17. Registration and payment for children and parents courses has to be done online through the links given on our website. Additional 400/- per child has to be paid in AOLCAMPS account as charges for outdoor activities . Take a screenshot of the payment receipt and upload in registration form.
